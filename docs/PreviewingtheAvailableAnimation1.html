Previewing the Available Animations

Want to see what each animation looks like? In the Project window, click on the Dummy_animation asset, then go to Model.



Click on the small arrow by the DummyAnim to expand its available selection.



Click on the breathe animation to open up the Animation Preview window:



Click on the Play button to watch the DummyAnim breath.


Once you've previewed each animation, and decide which ones you want, we can now add our individual animations.
