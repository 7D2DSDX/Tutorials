To Install:


Click on "Clone Or download" button, and click on Download Zip




On the downloaded Mods-master.zip file, extract the file



Copy some, or all the mods to your C:\SDXModding\SDX0.7.1\Targets\7DaysToDie\Mods folder



In the SDX Launcher, click on refresh to see the new Mods:
