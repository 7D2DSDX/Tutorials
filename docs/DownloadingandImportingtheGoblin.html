Downloading and Importing the Goblin robber

How Tos ›› How to set up the animated custom entity ››


It's a simple free object that has included animations.


In Unity, click on the Asset Store Tab



And searching for "Red Samuria". It should be the first hit in the search results




Click on the Goblin robber (free ),and click on the Download button:




Once downloaded, the Download link will change to "Import". Click on Import to add theRed Samurai to your new Unity Project.


The Import dialog box will pop up, showing everything included. Click on Import to bring them all into your project.



Once the Import is done, you'll see the new "C&C_Pack" folder, in your Project tab, under Assets.




Click on the Scene tab to get back to your starting area.
